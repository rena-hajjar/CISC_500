nnUNetv2_train DATASET_NAME_OR_ID UNET_CONFIGURATION FOLD [additional options, see -h]

UNET_CONFIGURATION=string that identifies the requested U-Net configuration (defaults: 2d, 3d_fullres, 3d_lowres, 3d_cascade_lowres).
DATASET_NAME_OR_ID=what dataset should be trained on
FOLD=which fold of the 5-fold-cross-validation is trained.

nnU-Net stores a checkpoint every 50 epochs. If you need to continue a previous training, just add a --c to the training command.

--val --npz -device cuda

nnUNetv2_train 555 2d 0 -device cuda
